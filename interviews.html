Vocabulary for interviews

In this section you will learn more vocabulary in order to express yourself better during an interview. So you will review some important and common phrases during an interview. A conversation will also be presented as an example of what has been learned.

Important words and phrases

Job interview: Is the proccess in which a potencial employee is evaluated by an employer through the asking and asnwering questions.

  • Why do you want to leave your present job?
  • How much would you hope to earn in this position?
  • Would you be willing to relocate/to move to another part of the country/to work abroad?
  • What aspects of the position are you most / least interested in?

I graduated from (university/college etc) in (the year)…
I studied at (university/college)…
I got a diploma in…. and then went on to study...
I am really good at showing initiative/presenting information/problem-solving/controlling budgets/achieving objectives/motivating colleagues/meeting deadlines/creating ideas.
I have very good people skills/management skills/organisational skills. Easy-going: a relaxed person who is easy to get along with.
Hard-working: someone who works well and isn’t lazy.
Committed: a person who is loyal to a project or person.
Trustworthy: someone who you can rely on.
Honest: someone who tells the truth.
Focused: someone who is not easily distracted.
Methodical: a person who pays attention to details and works in a logical way.
Proactive: someone who takes steps to complete tasks without supervision.



Before to continue studying phrases...

A tip for your next interview: As you answer questions, maintain eye contact with the interviewer. Be sure to pay attention to the question so that you don’t forget it, and listen to the entire question.

GOOD LUCK!


ANOTHER UNCOMMON words

Accomplishment – something that is done or achieved successfully. Leading the meeting in her supervisor’s absence was a big accomplishment.
Strength – a quality that someone or something has that helps them succeed. Many people like to say that working hard is their greatest strength.
Weakness – a quality someone or something has that is not good.
